AI SaaS Multi-Chatbot Platform

Problem

Teams needed multiple AI chatbots tailored to different use cases (support, internal ops, marketing) without duplicating infrastructure or prompts.

Technical approach

Built a multi-tenant SaaS platform allowing users to create, configure, and manage multiple AI chatbots from a single backend. Designed prompt templates, context isolation, and usage tracking to ensure accuracy and cost control. Implemented API-based orchestration with async processing and logging for reliability.

Tech stack

Python, Django, Django REST Framework, PostgreSQL (Supabase), OpenAI API, Celery, Redis, Docker.

Outcome / impact

Enabled rapid deployment of specialized chatbots, reduced manual support effort, and improved response consistency through structured prompts and centralized management.

Architecture & Thinking

  • APIs: versioned, predictable, and designed around business workflows, not endpoints.
  • Databases: schema first, enforce integrity with constraints, index for actual query patterns.
  • Pipelines: idempotent jobs, clear logs, and failure isolation for reliable automation.
